# AI Ethics Reflection

## Responsible AI Use

AI can help analysts move faster, but it can also create risk if users accept outputs without checking them. In this portfolio, AI is used as a support tool for brainstorming, drafting, explaining, and reviewing.

## Risks I Considered

- AI can overstate conclusions from limited data.
- AI can miss data quality problems.
- AI can produce recommendations that sound reasonable but are not supported by evidence.
- AI can hide uncertainty if prompts do not ask for limitations.

## My Approach

I used AI with human review. I checked claims against the dataset, separated observations from recommendations, and documented limitations where the evidence was incomplete.

## Key Lesson

Responsible AI use requires clear prompts, validation, transparency, and human accountability.
