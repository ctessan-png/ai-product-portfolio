# Speaker Notes

## Slide 1: Portfolio Overview

This portfolio shows how I use AI, Python, and Git to analyze business problems, create repeatable workflows, and communicate insights clearly.

## Slide 2: FoodHub EDA

The FoodHub project is the main technical project. I analyzed 1,898 orders to understand restaurant demand, cuisine popularity, delivery performance, ratings, and cost patterns.

## Slide 3: Key Findings

The strongest finding is that weekend demand is much higher than weekday demand. I also found that ratings are incomplete, with 736 orders marked as `Not given`, which affects how rating analysis should be interpreted.

## Slide 4: AI Prompt Engineering

The prompt engineering section shows that I used AI with structure. I improved broad prompts by adding role, task, context, constraints, and expected output.

## Slide 5: AI Business Use Case

The business use case designs an AI assistant for food delivery operations. The assistant summarizes order trends and flags risks, while keeping a human reviewer responsible for decisions.

## Slide 6: Reflection

The main takeaway is that AI is most valuable when combined with data validation, clear documentation, and business judgment.
