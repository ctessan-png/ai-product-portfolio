# Business Reflection

## What The Analysis Shows

The FoodHub dataset shows how order-level data can support business decisions. The analysis identifies demand concentration by day, restaurant, and cuisine type, while also showing where data quality limits interpretation.

## Business Value

The strongest business value comes from turning descriptive analysis into operating decisions:

- Weekend demand can guide staffing and delivery capacity.
- High-volume restaurants can be monitored for service quality.
- Cuisine popularity can guide promotions and partner strategy.
- Missing ratings can be treated as an improvement opportunity.

## How AI Supported The Work

AI was used as an assistant for:

- brainstorming business questions
- identifying data quality checks
- suggesting chart types
- explaining analysis steps
- improving documentation clarity
- challenging assumptions before finalizing recommendations

AI was not used as a replacement for validation. Outputs still needed to be checked against the dataset and business logic.

## Limitations

- The dataset does not include customer comments, refunds, cancellations, or exact order timestamps.
- Ratings are incomplete because many records are marked `Not given`.
- The analysis is exploratory and does not prove causation.
- Additional data would be needed to evaluate retention, customer lifetime value, or restaurant-level profitability.

## Next Steps

Future analysis could segment customers by repeat behavior, compare delivery performance by restaurant, and build a dashboard for weekly operational monitoring.
