# Problem Statement

Food delivery platforms need to quickly identify operational issues that affect customer satisfaction. These issues can include delayed deliveries, restaurants with inconsistent ratings, high-volume demand periods, and cuisine categories that need better supply coverage.

## Current Challenge

Operations teams often review reports manually. This can make it difficult to identify patterns quickly, especially when order volume is high.

## Users

- Operations managers
- Restaurant partnership managers
- Product analysts
- Customer experience teams

## Business Goal

Help teams make faster, evidence-based decisions about delivery performance, restaurant quality, demand planning, and customer satisfaction.

## Success Criteria

- Faster identification of restaurants or time periods with performance risk
- Clearer explanation of what data supports each recommendation
- Better prioritization of operational follow-up
- Improved ability to communicate insights to non-technical stakeholders
