# iPhone Widget Research Phase

## Project Purpose

This project begins the research phase for a future portfolio section focused on iPhone-style widgets inspired by personal hobbies, Pinterest visual design, and AI-assisted creativity.

This is not the build phase yet. The purpose of this section is to document the inspiration, the emotional reason behind the idea, and the early product direction before writing code.

## Core Idea

I want to explore how small digital widgets can make personal technology feel more useful, beautiful, and reflective. The inspiration comes from seeing highly styled iPhone widgets on Pinterest and realizing that a portfolio can show more than technical ability. It can also show taste, personality, creativity, and intention.

## Why This Belongs In My Portfolio

This project connects directly to the larger portfolio theme:

- AI can help turn vague inspiration into structured ideas.
- Design can make technical work feel personal and approachable.
- Widgets are small, focused product experiences.
- Personal hobbies can become meaningful technical artifacts.
- A portfolio can show how I think, not only what I code.

## Research Questions

1. What types of iPhone widgets are possible to create?
2. What visual styles appear most often in the Pinterest inspiration I like?
3. Which personal hobbies or daily routines would make meaningful widget concepts?
4. How can AI support ideation, design choices, copywriting, and reflection?
5. What would make these widgets feel useful instead of decorative only?

## Current Phase

Research and concept development.
